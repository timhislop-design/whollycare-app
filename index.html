Fragrance-free, paraben-free, sulfate-free, phthalate-free. Silicone-free. Formaldehyde-free. Any ingredient you avoid becomes a hard filter that removes products entirely — not just deprioritizes them.

Ethical Commitments

Cruelty-free, vegan, reef-safe, sustainably sourced. These aren't "nice to have" filters — they're constraints that run first. No brand pays to bypass them. The Sincere Strategy doesn't allow it.

Hypoallergenic, non-comedogenic, eczema-safe, sensitive skin formulated. Specific allergen avoidance (lanolin, propylene glycol, etc.). Dermatologist-tested flags. What your skin needs is a constraint, not a filter preference.
