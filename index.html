How It Works

Your values filter first.
Savings follow.

01

Set your profile

Your ingredient avoidances, ethical commitments, and skin sensitivity flags. These never change without your explicit action — they're constraints, not defaults to be overridden.

02

Constraint engine runs

Every product across every store is checked against your profile. Anything that fails is removed from results — not deprioritized, not ranked lower. Removed entirely.

03

Cross-store pricing

What passes your profile is cross-compared across CVS, Walgreens, Ulta, and Target — by unit price (per fl oz, per count) — not manipulated by package size.

04

Your care list

One list, per store, with net savings shown — loyalty discounts and shipping costs factored in. The number in your pocket, not the number that flatters the recommendation.
