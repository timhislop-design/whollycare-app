Ingredient Intelligence

The label says "gentle."
The ingredient list tells the truth.

WhollyCare reads every ingredient list — not just the marketing claims. Your sensitivity profile is a hard filter. Products that fail it are removed before any price comparison begins. You never see a product that isn't safe for you.

🌿
Fragrance sensitivities
"Fragrance" is a legal catch-all for hundreds of undisclosed chemicals. WhollyCare flags products with fragrance for sensitivity profiles — not just perfume-free label claims.
🧪
Sulfate & silicone tracking
SLS, SLES, dimethicone, cyclomethicone — tracked by INCI name, not marketing label. "Natural" on the front does not override the ingredient list on the back.
⚗️
Preservative profiles
Parabens, phenoxyethanol, formaldehyde-releasing agents — each tracked individually. Your profile can exclude specific preservatives while allowing others.
🌱
Values-based filtering
Cruelty-free, vegan, reef-safe, EWG-verified — WhollyCare applies values filters the same way it applies sensitivity filters. Hard constraints, not suggestions.

How It Works

Safety filter first.
Price comparison second.

01
Build your care profile
Skin type, known sensitivities, ingredient exclusions, values (vegan, cruelty-free, etc.), and the stores you actually shop. One profile per person in your household.
02
Ingredient check runs first
Every product in the comparison is filtered against your profile before any price is shown. Products that fail are removed — with a reason logged, visible to you.
03
See what's safe and affordable
Only products that pass your profile appear. Prices pulled from CVS, Walgreens, Target, Ulta, Walmart, and Amazon. Unit price per ounce, always.
04
Shop with confidence
Your shopping list is pre-filtered. No reading ingredient lists in the aisle. No second-guessing. The work was done before you left the house.
